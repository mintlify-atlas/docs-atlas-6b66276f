## About this project

This repository was created **by students, for students** of the Information Technology program at **IBMR** — and is open to students from any university who want to learn or practice the **C programming language**.

You'll find exercises and examples organized by topic, covering everything from the basics to more advanced subjects. The goal is to serve as support material for beginners and as a quick reference for those with some experience.
